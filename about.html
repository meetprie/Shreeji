Company Introduction

SHREEJI AL SHAMAL GHANA LTD is incorporated in 2011 by the very experienced and highly motivated persons with the motto of providing the best ever servicing in the field of Mining and Construction industries as a quick, easy and reliable destination with affordable rate.

We have started our operation in Ghana and now we have strong hold in mining and construction industries as rental and service provider. We are a well-known rental company in our region and have customers with various work orders from across the country. Basically our roots spread in West Africa but now our vision is spreading roots globally. Also our sister concerns companies are Shreeji AL Shamal Ivory – Cote D’ivoire, Dhruvi Impex Inc. – USA, Dhruvi Impex Pvt. Ltd. – India & Dhruvi Impex F.Z.E. – U.A.E.

Our Vision
